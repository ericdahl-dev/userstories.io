After submitting a story, collaborators can optionally refine it in a short AI chat. The assistant uses project history and linked repository context to ask clarifying questions and tighten the narrative before the story lands in the developer triage inbox.

Highlights:

- Turbo-streamed replies for a responsive chat feel
- Reply cap to keep refinement focused
- Finalized story flows into the existing triage workflow unchanged
